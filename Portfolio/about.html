About Me

Entrepreneur | Media Mogul | Creative Director

Malibe Mahao is a multi-faceted professional whose diverse expertise spans digital marketing, entertainment, and media. With a career built on innovation and excellence, Malibe has established himself as a leading figure in Lesotho's creative and business landscapes.

His unique ability to identify opportunities and transform them into successful ventures has made him a respected name across multiple industries. From revolutionizing digital advertising through WhatsApp to establishing prestigious awards in the music industry, Malibe's impact is both broad and profound.

Professional Journey

  • 2010–2013: Began career in traditional marketing, developing foundational skills in advertising and brand promotion.
  • 2014: Launched innovative WhatsApp advertising solutions.
  • 2015: Founded the Lesotho Music Awards.
  • 2017: Created Melodscape Magazine, an online entertainment platform.
  • 2018–Present: Serves as a beauty pageant judge and professional stage manager.

Professional Philosophy

  • Innovation: Constantly seeking creative ways to solve problems and generate value.
  • Integrity: Building trust through transparency and ethical business practices.
  • Impact: Leading initiatives that create meaningful change in communities and industries.
